HammAli&Navai

About Artist

HammAli and Navai(Alexander Aliev and Navai Bakirov) are young Russian performers working at the intersection of pop and rap genres. The first hit of young artists was the song "You are my chemistry", which was included in the top 20 most listened to songs in Russia, and the success of the joint single "Fly Together" with Bahh Tee surpassed the success of the song "You are my chemistry", forcing the attention of large publications to young artists and labels. In two days, the video was viewed more than 2 million times, and the song was included in the twenty most listened to Russian-language songs in Russia - today the artists presented the video "Fly Together" on YouTube. Note that the song "Fly Together" was included in the new Bahh Tee album "Can You", which was released on April 21st.
